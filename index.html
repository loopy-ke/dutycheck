How KRA calculates import duty

KRA uses the official CRSP (Current Retail Selling Price) list as the starting value for every car. The CRSP is adjusted by a KRA factor and the car's age to arrive at the Customs Value, from which five levies are charged.

CRSP × (1 − depreciation%) ÷ 2.4469 = Customs Value (CV)
Import DutyCV × 25%
Excise Duty(CV + ID) × 20%
VAT(CV + ID + ED) × 16%
IDFCV × 2.25% (min KES 5,000)
RDLCV × 1.5%
